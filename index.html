Working Together is Important

Why Working Together is Important

There are many divides in the world. They seem to be getting bigger and bigger each day. People are constantly fighting eachother on who's right and what to do.

My UN goal was Peace, Justice, and Strong Institutions. I believe that by showing people the benefit of working together, we may come closer to achieving that goal.

The game above may show you how working together can help both sides of a party. It is based off of the story of the Tortoise and the Hare which you can view here. The twist in this game is that the tortoise and the hare have to work together to progress in the game.
